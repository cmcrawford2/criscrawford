Trip to Argentina, May 2017

I bought a tour to an unknown destination. The destination was revealed to me on the day that I was scheduled to leave. I was going to spend two weeks in Argentina, much of that time touring the Atacama Plateau in the Andes of Northern Argentina.

Day 1: I arrived in Buenos Aires late, which apparently threw things off and caused a bit of a scramble. The theme was Buenos Aires 101. First stop coffee at Cafe Tortoni, then a tour of the Boca district with lunch at El Obrero, and finally Plaza Mayo. I skipped supper

Day 2: Boated up the delta in a lancha to visit the school Primaria #13 in San Fernando. I was so honored to be the esteemed visitor. The kids had made me cards. Dinner party with women from the office. Very nice.

Day 3: Left for Salta in the morning. Met Phillipe briefly at the hotel. Had a tour of Salta with Gonzalo who was to be my guide for the next week. MAAM museum, cathedral, plaza with orange trees. Hotel was the awesome House of Jasmines
